Instructions For Usage

  • Login \ Logout

    • To login click the GREEN colored login button on the homepage of the web-application. It would redirect you to the keycloak login page. Here you can register yourself if not already, else login with your details. After successful login you would see the homepage again, with more button on the navigation bar to use the web-application.
    • To logout click the RED colored logout button, that would appear after you have successfully logged-in, on the homepage of the web-application. After that extra buttons would disappear, not allowing you to manipulate the To-Do list.

  • List all To-Do \ List a To-Do

    • To list all To-Do after the login, click the 'List all To-Do', it would take you to 'list all To-Do' page, where you would find a table, listing all the details of the To-Dos available. The details are: Title, Description, Time and Images (for pro users only). This table would consist of an extra column naming options which would give you facility to edit or delete any task.
    • To list a To-Do after the login, click the 'List a To-Do', it would take you to 'list a To-Do' page, where you would find an input field to enter the To-Do title, after clicking the 'submit' button, it would display the To-Do below in a table format (if exist). The details are: Title, Description, Time and Images (for pro users only). This table would consist of an extra column naming options which would give you facility to edit or delete any task.
      The input field for title would still be displayed after the search result, just to search another To-Do.
    • Non-Pro License Users would see the Images column in the table filled with 'None'.
      Pro License Users which have not added any images, would see the same.

  • Add a To-Do

    • To add a To-Do after the login, click the 'Add a To-Do' and enter the details of the To-Do. Pro users would also be able to add images in their To-Dos. After successful addition of the To-Do into the database, you would be redirected to 'List A To-Do' page, where you would see your recently created To-Do. The details that would be asked for creating a new To-Do would be: Title, Description, Time and Images (for pro users only) .
      NOTE: TITLE FOR EACH AND EVERY TO-DO ARE UNIQUE, MAKE SURE TO ENTER UNIQUE TITLE, FOR EVERY TO-DO, IN ORDER TO SUCCESSFUL CREATION OF TO-DO, ELSE IT'LL FAIL.

  • Edit a To-Do

    • To edit a To-Do after the login, click the 'List all To-Do' or 'List a To-Do' (and search for specific To-Do) after that click the 'edit' button in the 'Options' column of the table. This will redirect you to the 'edit a To-Do' page, where the form would be pre-filled by the previous data of the To-Do, allowing you to manipulate the description, time and images (for pro users only). After the changes click the 'Modify' button below, and it would redirect you to the 'List all To-Do', where you would be able to see the changes you modified.

  • Delete a To-Do

    • To delete a To-Do after the login, click the 'List all To-Do' or 'List a To-Do' (and search for specific To-Do) after that click the 'delete' button in the 'Options' column of the table. This will delete the To-Do from the database, and you can see the changes on the spot.

  • Pro License

    • To get a Pro License and able to add images to you To-Dos, click on the sky-blue Buy Pro License button in the navigation bar. This would redirect you to the 'Stripe' payment gateway, where you would be able to subscribe to the To-Do Pro License.
      After the successful payment, it would take you to the Thank-you page, ensuring your confirmation of the subscription.
      If the payment is failed due to any of the issues, it would redirect you to the retry payment page from where you can go to home page and retry the payment of the subscription.
      Once the payment is successful, you would be a Pro Licensed user until the subscription ends. Also, the sky-blue 'Buy Pro License' would be converted into the 'YOU ARE PRO LICENSED button.
